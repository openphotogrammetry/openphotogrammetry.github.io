3D Model Gallery
These 3D models were created from short video clips using our photogrammetry pipeline. The 3D scenes are rendered using 3D gaussian splatting. The online viewer is adapted from GaussianSplats3D (thanks to the author for sharing his work!). The input video clips are from the Tanks and Temples 3D reconstruction benchmark.

Click on each 3D scene in the gallery to interactively view them. The viewer uses the following mouse inputs:
  • Left click to set the focal point
  • Left click and drag to orbit
  • Right click and drag to pan
